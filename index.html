Course introduction

Hello! I’m Thicha

Hello! I’m Thicha from Chiang Mai, Thailand — a native Thai teacher with experience teaching students from many different countries, age groups, and language levels.

I began my teaching journey working in international school environments before moving into private tutoring and online Thai classes. Over the years, I’ve taught students from complete beginners to more advanced learners, always focusing on helping them build confidence in speaking and understanding Thai naturally step by step.

I hold a professional teaching certification, and my lessons are practical, friendly, and tailored to each student’s goals and learning style. Many of my students have found me through personal recommendations and word of mouth from learners around the world.

Beyond language, I also enjoy sharing Thai culture to make learning feel more natural, meaningful, and enjoyable. My goal is to create a supportive and comfortable environment where students can truly enjoy learning Thai with confidence.
